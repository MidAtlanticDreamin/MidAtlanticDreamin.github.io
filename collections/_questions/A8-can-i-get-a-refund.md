---
question: "Is there a deadline to get a refund for my ticket?"
section: "General"
order: 8
code: G6
---

You can receive a full refund for your event ticket purchase as long as you request it at least 7 days in advance of the date of the event. No refund is available when there are less than 7 days until the event or the event has already taken place. You can reach out to us at [info@midatlanticdreamin.com](mailto:info@midatlanticdreamin.com) for more details.
